Notice

Please note that the BMI calculator is just a suggestion and not to be the only health calculator you should consider. other factors need to be taken into account. See your GP or the NHS website for more details.

Underweight

Being underweight could be a sign you're not eating enough or you may be ill. If you're underweight, a GP can help.

Healthy weight

Keep up the good work! For tips on maintaining a healthy weight, check out the food and diet and fitness sections.

Overweight

The best way to lose weight if you're overweight is through a combination of diet and exercise.

The BMI calculator will give you a personal calorie allowance to help you achieve a healthy weight safely.

Obese

The best way to lose weight if you're obese is through a combination of diet and exercise, and, in some cases, medicines. See a GP for help and advice.

Morbidly obese

The best way to lose weight if you're morbidly obese is through a combination of diet and exercise, and medicines. See a GP for help and advice. This is an extremely dangerous place to be.

Why should you eat healthily?

Eating well is fundamental to good health and well-being. Healthy eating helps you to maintain a healthy weight and reduces your risk of type 2 diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol and the risk of developing cardiovascular disease and some cancers. As well as this a good diet can improve your mental well being making you still feel active and not sluggish. Furthermore sertain foods that contain vitamins like B12, D and C can improve your immune system or increase your energy.
